About Us

We are experienced educators – Renadheer and Ramanathan – launching a fully online 6-month coaching program for MSc Physics aspirants. Our classes are structured, topic-focused, and led by alumni from NIT/IIT. We prepare students for major MSc entrance exams including JAM, CUET-PG, JEST, TIFR GS, and NEST, covering both central university and elite institute-level admissions.

  • Weekly 4-hour interactive classes
  • Concept-oriented practice worksheets
  • Intensive exam series tailored for IIT JAM, JEST, CUET-PG, TIFR GS, and NEST — covering the most competitive MSc Physics entrances in India
  • Personalized doubt clearing and problem-solving sessions
